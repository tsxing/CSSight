Neumorphism

Gives an extruded "soft 3D" effect to elements, making them look as if they're subtly raised or recessed from the background.

Glassmorphism

Mimics appearance of glass. Creates an elegant aesthetic by making elements appear as if they're semi-transparent layers of stacked glass.
